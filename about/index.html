About Prostor

The idea of Prostor (translates as 'space' in English) started at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana some time ago. We wanted to create a joint website that serves as an archive and access point for open source projects from our university. We've gathered a solid amount of such projects and we're happy to showcase them here.

All projects here are licensed under one of the open source licences (GPL, MIT, CC...) and are free to download, use, resue and, especially, contribute to.

Together we're making open source stronger and better!
